Your Decision Guide

This is the fundamental choice between the "mom-and-pop" feeling of direct ownership and the "systems over chaos" reality of protection and simplicity. Here are the four options.

Option 1: Direct 6-Partner Ownership (The "Mom-and-Pop" Way)

Structure: IE has NO ownership. The 6 partners own GGP directly.

Consequences (The Chaos):

  • Tax Nightmare: GGP becomes a Partnership with its own 1065 and K-1s.
  • Governance Chaos: New GGP Operating Agreement; 6-way voting/gridlock risk.
  • Weakened Connection: Legal friction with FFC, MOHV, GGCG.

Option 2: Hybrid Ownership (The "Worst of Both Worlds")

Structure: IE owns majority; one+ partners own minority directly.

Consequences:

  • Still a Tax Return: Multiple owners → Partnership filing anyway.
  • Still Governance Complexity: Rights/votes between IE vs minority.
  • Breaks Simplicity: Complexity ↑, protection ≠ stronger.

Option 3: Parallel Holding Co. ("Grumpy Goat Holdings")

Structure: New LLC owned by the 6 partners owns 100% of GGP, separate from IE.

Consequences:

  • Violates Unity: Creates a silo outside IE’s fortress.
  • Tax Complexity: New partnership return at the holding co.
  • Governance Duplication: Parallel board, duplicated overhead.

Option 4: The Designed Fortress (Systems Over Chaos)

Structure: Infinite Earth owns 100% of GGP (SMLLC, disregarded).

Benefits:

  • Max Tax Simplicity: No separate GGP return; rolls up to IE.
  • Clean Governance: One board, one playbook, clear execution.
  • Strongest Protection: Fully integrated with IE/FFC/MOHV/GGCG shields.

The fundamental choice is:

Do we choose the feeling of direct ownership and accept the reality of tax nightmares and governance chaos?

OR

Do we choose the system of unified ownership to gain the reality of tax simplicity, clean governance, and true, lasting protection?
